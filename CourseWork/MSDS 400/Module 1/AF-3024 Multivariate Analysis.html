AF-3024 Multi-variate Regression Analysis¶

The goal of this analysis is to understand the key influencers on the AF-3024 Unit Cost produced in Springfield. Because this product is being discussed as providing a manufacturing blueprint for the New Core Splice NPI material, a better understanding of the cost implications is needed.¶

Pairplot PM-7398¶

In [9]:
sns.pairplot(ucPM7398f,
            x_vars = ['Raw Material Price','Personnel Price','Overhead/Machine Price','QTY'],
            y_vars = ['Unit Cost'],
            kind = "reg",
            height = 4)
plt.show()

PM-7398 shows a strong positive correlation between Unit Cost and Overhead/Machine Price. This seems to be the dominant factor on the PM-7398 unit cost.

There are slightly positive correlations between Unit Cost and Personnel Price along with Raw Material Price, but not as strong as Overhead/Machine Price

There is a weak negative correlation between Unit Cost and QTY.

Pairplot EC-3025 Jumbo¶

In [11]:
sns.pairplot(ucEC3025f,

            y_vars = ['Unit Cost'],

The EC-3025 Jumbo data shows much stronger correlations with the 4 variables analyzed. The Personnel Price and Overhead/Machine Price seem to have similar strength postivie correlations with Unit Cost. The Raw Material Price has a more pronounced positive correlation compared to the PMs and there is a more defined negative correlation with QTY.

This indicates that the run length is having an impact on EC-3025 jumbo Unit Cost compared to the PMs made on the moguls and pony mixers.
